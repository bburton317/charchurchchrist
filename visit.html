Visit Us

In worship assemblies the holy scriptures are read, studied, and expounded upon. Psalms, hymns, and spiritual songs are sung by the entire congregation. Prayers are offered to God the Father through and in the name of Jesus Christ. Members voluntarily contribute to support the ministry of the church. The Lord's Supper is observed weekly in remembrance of the crucified and risen Christ. We welcome you to assemble and worship with us.

In Bible studies we offer classes for all ages from preschool to adult. The Word of God is studied and discussed. Like the Bereans (Acts 17:11-11), we spend this time "examining the Scriptures" to discover the truth. Click here to view our current quarter of classes. We invite you to come study with us.
